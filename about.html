Our Story

What started as a dream to share the beauty of Karnataka's culture with the world has blossomed into Paramathma Stays. Our family has been hosting guests for over a decade, creating memorable experiences and lasting friendships.

We take pride in maintaining the authentic charm of a traditional Karnataka home while providing all modern amenities for a comfortable stay. Our commitment to personalized service and attention to detail has earned us numerous accolades and the loyalty of guests who return year after year.
